Vegetables

Vegetables are the edible parts of plants
providing essential vitamins, minerals, fiber,
and antioxidants, and are categorized based on their plant part



Fruits

Fruits are seed-bearing structures
that grow from a flower's ovary,
serving to contain and disperse the plant's seeds.



Nuts

Nuts offer a range of health benefits
due to their rich nutrient profile, including healthy
fats, protein, fiber, and various vitamins and minerals.
